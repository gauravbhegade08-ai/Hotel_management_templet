A Legacy in Stone

Built in 1743, our palace stands as a testament to Rajputana architecture.

Welcome to a sanctuary where history breathes through every corridor. The Royal Heritage is not merely a hotel; it is a meticulously restored 18th-century palace that once housed nobility.

Every courtyard, every hand-painted fresco, and every intricately carved jali screen tells a story of an era where craftsmanship was paramount and hospitality was considered a sacred duty. We invite you to live the legend.
